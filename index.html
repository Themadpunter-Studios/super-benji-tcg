What Is It

The Super Benji Trading Card Game

The objective is simple: defeat your opponent by lowering their health before they lower yours. Play Characters from the show and movies to clash on the battlefield, then shift momentum with clever Actions, game-changing Locations, and powerful Artifacts.

Story

Factions Of Benji Island

Two factions are locked in a battle for control of Benji Island: Benji and his heroes, the League of Grace, against Dr. Banana and his villains, the Banana Republic. Every deck and duel reflects this larger struggle for power across the island.

Core Gameplay

How Victory Works

1. Summon Characters

Deploy heroes, villains, and allies from the Super Benji universe to pressure your opponent.

2. Control The Field

Use Actions, Locations, and Artifacts to disrupt combos, buff your side, and swing combat outcomes.

3. Claim Benji Island

Keep pushing damage and tactical advantages until your opponent's health is fully depleted.
